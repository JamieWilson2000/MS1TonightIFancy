Perfect Grilled Steak

Try out this delicious Perfect Grilled Steak bought to us by our friends at

delish.com
with tutorial by
Jamie Oliver

Serves: 4
Prep time: 15 mins
Total time: 1hr 55mins

  • Ingredients

  • 1/4 c. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 c. lemon juice (from 2 lemons)
  • 2 tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tbsp. packed brown sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp. fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 1/2 lb. hanger steak
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper

  • Method

  • 1. In a baking dish, whisk together oil, lemon juice, Worcestershire, sugar, and garlic in a bowl. Place steak in marinade and turn to coat. Cover with plastic. Let stand, turning steak occasionally, at least 1 hour at room temperature, or up to 4 hours in refrigerator.
  • 2. Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate. Remove meat from marinade and pat off excess with paper towels. Season with salt and pepper.
  • 3. Grill, turning once, until medium-rare, about 5 minutes per side.
  • 4. Transfer to a cutting board and let stand 10 minutes. Slice meat against the grain.
